Pipodoro

Extremely lightweight Pomodoro app that has PiP and AirPlay functionality on MacOS/iOS. Canvas and video elements are used to create the timer. Installable and works offline (PWA) through the use of a Service Worker. Whole app is less than 3kb. Zero dependencies. No build step.
